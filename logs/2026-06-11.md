- DC (VTC): Classic inverter S-curve

  - Sharp transition region

## Concepts Reinforced
- `vpulse`: V1 (low), V2 (high), period, rise/fall time, delay
- ADE L workflow: Setup → Analyses → Outputs → Run → Results
- VTC tells you: VOH, VOL, VIH, VIL, noise margins, VM
