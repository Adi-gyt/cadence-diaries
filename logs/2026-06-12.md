# Day 1 — June 12, 2026

**Focus:** Inverter schematic → symbol → testbench → transient → DC → delay → PVT corners

## What I did

### Schematic
- Created `inverter` cell in `project0` library
- Placed PMOS (`pmos_18`) and NMOS (`nmos_18`) from SCL 180nm PDK
- PMOS: W=2µm, L=0.18µm, nf=1, m=1
- NMOS: W=1µm, L=0.18µm, nf=1, m=1
- Connected: gates → VIN, drains → VOUT, PMOS source → vdd, NMOS source → VSS

### Symbol
- Created symbol view from schematic
- Pins: VIN (input), VOUT (output), vdd, VSS

### Testbench (`inverter_tb`)
- Instantiated inverter symbol
- `V0` (vsource): pulse input, 0→1.8V
- `V1` (vdc): VDD = 1.8V
- `C0` (cap): load capacitor at output
- Used ADE L for simulation setup

### Transient Simulation
- Input: pulse, 0→1.8V, period ~40ns
- Plotted VOUT (red) and net1/VIN (green)
- Output correctly inverts input, rail-to-rail swing

### DC Simulation (VTC)
- Swept VIN from 0 to 1.8V
- VTC curve shows sharp transition around ~0.9V (VM ≈ VDD/2)
- Both transient and DC results viewed side by side

### Propagation Delay
- Used waveform markers on transient output
- Measured at 50% crossing points (900mV = VDD/2)
- **tpLH** (output low→high): ~4.286ns (marker: dx=4.285867ns)
- **tpHL** (output high→low): ~3.276ns
- Also measured using ADE calculator

### PVT Corner Analysis (ADE XL)
- Ran across 12 corners: Nominal, FF, FS, SF, SS (at temps -40, 27, 120°C)
- Process corners from `ts18sl_scl.lib`
- Outputs tracked: /VOUT, /net1, tpLH, tpHL
- Results:
  - tpLH nominal: -4.286ns | min: -6.171ns | max: -3.162ns
  - tpHL nominal: -3.276ns | min: -4.523n | max: -2.586ns
- Plotted tpLH and tpHL vs temperature across corners
- SS corner (slow process, high temp) = worst case delay

## Key Observations
- Delay increases at SS corner and high temperature — transistors are slower
- FF corner at low temp = fastest (best case)
- PMOS sized 2x NMOS to balance rise/fall times
